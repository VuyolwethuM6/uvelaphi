Discover Your African Roots with Uvelaphi

Uvelaphi is a web and mobile platform dedicated to reconnecting African people with their heritage through the knowledge of African clan names. Our mission is to create an intergenerational reservoir of history, ensuring the stories and traditions of African people are preserved for future generations.

Our Mission

We empower African people by providing comprehensive knowledge about African clan names, fostering a deep sense of identity and belonging.
